# Waterflow Support

Waterflow is an iOS app for monitoring USGS water stations — real-time streamflow, gauge height, and related hydrological data sourced from the U.S. Geological Survey's public Water Services API.

## Get Help

For questions, bug reports, or feature requests, email:

**steelhead@thundergrouse.com**

I aim to respond within 2–3 business days.

## When emailing, please include

- Your iOS version (Settings → General → About → iOS Version)
- Your iPhone or iPad model
- The Waterflow app version (Settings tab inside the app)
- A short description of what you were doing and what went wrong
- A screenshot, if relevant

## Data Source

Waterflow uses public data from the [USGS Water Services API](https://waterservices.usgs.gov/). USGS data is provisional and subject to revision.
